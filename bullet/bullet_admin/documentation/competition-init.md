# Creating new competition

1. Open the "Competition switcher" by clicking on the two arrows at the top of the
  sidebar.
2. Click on "New competition" button.
3. Fill out competition details:

- **Year:** The edition number of the competition (e.g., 4 for the 4th Náboj).
- **Web start:** The date from which the website starts displaying dates, teams
  and other details of this competition.
- **Registration start:** The date when registration opens.
- **Registration second round start:** The date when the second round of registration
  opens. Leave blank if you won't be using a second round.
- **Registration end:** The date when registration closes.
- **Competition start:** The date when the global competition begins.
- **Competition duration:** Duration of the competition in HH:MM:SS format.
- **Results freeze:** How long before the competition ends the public results
  freeze (HH:MM:SS format).
- **Problem count:** The number of problems in the competition. You can leave this
  as 0 and change it later.

Once you have this new competition, switch to it and configure categories.

## Setting up categories

1. Open "Categories" in the sidebar.
2. Click "New category".
3. Fill out category details:

- **Identifier:** The lowercase name of the category used internally by the system.
  (One of `junior`, `senior`, `nj`).
- **Order:** Categories are sorted by this number in ascending order when displayed.
- **First problem:** The number of the first problem for this category.
- **Available problems:** The number of problems a team has at any given time.
- **Max. number of team members:** The maximum number of members allowed per team.
- **Max. number of teams per school:** The maximum number of teams a school can
  register. Any additional teams will be placed on the waiting list. This only applies
  during the 1st round of registration.
- **Max. number of teams per school (2nd round):** The maximum number of teams a
  school can register during the 2nd round of registration. Put in 0 if you won't
  be using the second round.
- **Allowed school grades:** The school grades that can participate in this category
  (select multiple options).
